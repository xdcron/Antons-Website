Wonder Women Namibia

Background

Wonder Women Charity Organization, an organization that is more driven in helping young girls and women to discover their purpose, passion, identity and self-worthy. This is achieving through training, talk shows, donating clothes, period pads and foods to vulnerable women and girls. And offer psychosocial support and all possible way. The talk was designed to reach larger audience, inspire women through testimonies of other women who have been in their similar situation and came out strong, and whatever is raised through this talk show, goes out to the community to help women and girls.

Vision

Striving for purpose driven life” to enhance holistic lives of young girls, ladies and women through mentorship, coaching and training. Discovering, Developing and Deploying The power and purpose of woman.

    Vision Action Indicators

  • To educate, encourage and bring people to their realization of their God’s given purpose of their lives.
  • To inform, educate and establish a strong faith in our Namibian People through Community services
  • To be an agent of change in our Era where the world in drowning in social media pressure.
  • To maintain and develop a strong community with strong and right attitude towards our daily lives.
  • To be a mediator or initiate the contribution from anyone willing to help so we can help those that need medical attention as well as those that want to further their studies and they are from disadvantaged background.

Above all, we just want to bring love, hope and encourage people that through faith all is possible. The world has more people hurting but we are more concerned about our materials things than to be there for each other.

Mission

To recognize, develop, equip potentials and promote the strength of girls between the age of 10-15, and young adults between the age of 15 -30 in life with full reliance on our creator while fulfilling our own purpose in life. Motivate and encourage youths to always pursue on becoming better version of themselves.

  • Create a social media platform/pages, and use blogs, drama, dance, poetry, music in spaces where women and men will engage and discuss issues currently happening in our societies.
  • Training and teaching girls and women on self-value.
  • Encourage women not to give up on their marriages and know that God does not make a mistake when you marry.
  • To assist women, realize their purpose on earth, as well as encouraging others that through faith we can achieve everything we desire in life and that nothing is impossible with GOD.
  • To promote peace and harmony in marriages by hosting successful marriage talk shows, and promote relationship with God in our homes.
  • To mentor young women and men to become responsible citizens of tomorrow.
  • To arrange seminars, trainings, workshops and focus group discussion to educate, encourage one another to identify and focus on our purpose for we are here for a purpose
  • TV shows on real talk with people that are willing to share about their life and teach the young ones what they need to know when faced with certain issues/challenges in their lives.
  • Bring hope, healing and encouragement/ motivation through the word of GOD.
  • Documenting life stories of women who have brought positive change in our societies and country at large
  • Invite remarkable people that have survived gender based violence and focused on the future so they give hope/reassurance to others that have experienced it and have lost hope.
  • Formal and informal teaching, knowledge sharing forums to offer support to heal and comfort traumatized women who are affected in various ways, capacity building, spread the impact of education and offer more learning opportunity for the drop out due to teenage pregnancies.
  • Educate young girls on hygiene and Sanitation, empowerment-life skills e.g handicraft/tailoring and marketing, décor and catering as well as beauty products.
  • Humanitarian Assistance in terms of relief, food distribution and expensive treatment process.

Our Talk Show

    Aim of Our Talk show

  • Transforming young girls and women through hosting guest who have gone through the darkest moments and came out shining as stars. Using and sharing their stories on this platform to give hope and inspiration to young and old women who feel hopeless about life and their situation.
  • To bring closer and create an open platform where real life experiences, skills and knowledge and be shared, there is a battle of hierarchy and gender inequality affecting many women in our society and this will be a platform to get skills and learn from others women how to excel in such a society.
  • To reach a larger audience and impact more lives, because television can reach a wider range of people in all from classes, tribe and religion
  • Introduce the talk show to the Namibian and African audience, to tell the Namibia story and share experience that resonate with Namibians and Africans. To educate, inspire, motivate and share love and laughter with our audience.
  • Namibia at the moment has no TV talk show that is exclusively targeted at young girls and women empowerment, we do have several shows in the country but mainly mixed, and none that exclusively addresses women issues.
